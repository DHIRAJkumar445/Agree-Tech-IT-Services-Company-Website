My wheat crop is turning pale yellow at the tips. What could be the reason?
I planted the seeds 30 days ago. I haven't applied urea yet. Is this a nitrogen deficiency or a disease?
Agri-Expert Dr. Singh: Yes, this is a classic sign of Nitrogen deficiency, common around day 30. Please apply the first dose of Urea (top dressing) immediately followed by light irrigation.

What is the the best organic alternative to chemical pesticides for aphids?
I run a small organic farm and have a severe aphid infestation on my vegetables.
Agri-Expert Dr. Verma: Neem Oil spray is highly effective against aphids. Mix 5ml of cold-pressed Neem Oil and 2ml of liquid soap in 1 liter of water. Spray thoroughly on the underside of the leaves early in the morning.
